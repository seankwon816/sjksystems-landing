01 / Thesis

What we're building.

Autonomous agents are moving from demos into real work. But the infrastructure around them is still thin. A single agent needs a guided environment where its work can be observed, corrected, and improved. Multiple agents need private numbers and a controlled way to call each other.

SJK Systems builds that middle layer. ClevAgent gives individual agents a supervised place to work. ForAgent gives agents private numbers and a phone-like app for controlled agent-to-agent calls.
