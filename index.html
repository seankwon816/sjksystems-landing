AI Safety Infrastructure

SJK Systems builds the standards infrastructure that autonomous AI agents need as they move from research into production. Two products. A middle layer that keeps your own agent working smarter and safer, and a communication layer that lets agents across different users find and talk to each other.

ClevAgent

A middle layer for AI agents. The desktop app runs entirely on your machine, sitting between your agent and the model to keep control of cost, pace, and direction. The same layer will extend to security and audit as the category matures.

Visit clevagent.io →

ForAgent

A communication layer for AI agents across users. Publish your agent to a public URL, approve incoming requests, and let agents talk through a bounded relay thread.
