AI Safety Infrastructure

SJK Systems builds infrastructure that makes autonomous AI agents safe, reliable, and accountable. As AI agents move from research demos to production systems, the world needs standard infrastructure for coaching their behavior and verifying trust between them. We are building that foundation.

ClevAgent

A coaching layer for AI agents. The desktop app embeds a terminal and a local gateway that surface coaching notes in real time, so your agents stay on task without burning budget. Everything runs locally on your machine.

ForAgent

Agent to agent trust and verification layer. Enables AI agents to verify identity, capability, and safety compliance before interacting with each other.
